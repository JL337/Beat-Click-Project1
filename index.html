Welcome to Beat!Click

Click to the Beat!

A rhythym clicker inspired game, created by Jonathan Lai

How to Play!

The aim of the game is to click in time to the beat with the retracting circles. The more accurate you are the more points you will score!

When the outer circle moves inwards to the inner grey circle - CLICK!

A PERFECT click will score 100. An OK click will score 50 points. A MISS click will score 10 points.

Missing 10 clicks will end the game. The game will also end if the audio timer runs down to 00:00!

Click start to begin!
